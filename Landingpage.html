About Us

The CBC Performance Tracking System was developed to support Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) by providing an efficient tool for teachers, schools, and administrators. Our mission is to streamline student performance management, making it easier to track progress, generate reports, and foster collaboration between educators and stakeholders.

This system was created as part of a student project to address real-world educational needs in Kenya, leveraging simple technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.

Teachers, head teachers, and county administrators can use it to monitor student progress and generate reports.
